What is Aspora?
Core definition — memorise thisAspora is operated by Real Transfer Limited (RTL) — formerly known as Vance, rebranded to Aspora, backed by Sequoia Capital. RTL is an FCA-authorised payment institution (UK) for money remittance only. RTL is NOT a bank — not PRA-regulated, does not accept UK deposits.

  • 💸Transfers to 3,000+ Indian banks · matches Google exchange rates · zero hidden FX fees · bill payments, EMIs, SIPs automation
  • ⚠️Key risk to disclose: exchange rate fluctuations may affect deposit value when converted to GBP; deposits subject to Indian law
  • 🌍Currently live: UK · US · UAE · EU — 100% digital, ~15 min to open, zero fees, zero minimum balance

📢 30-second explanation for customers"NRE is for money you earn abroad — tax-free in India, fully repatriable, earns 7%. NRO is for income from India like rent or pension — taxable at 30% TDS, capped repatriation. Most NRIs working abroad primarily need NRE."
💸
NRO Repatriation — The Process
Customers often ask — know the steps
⚠️ Annual limit: USD 1 million per financial year (April–March)Tax clearance is mandatory before transfer.
  • 1️⃣Check remaining FY limit
  • 2️⃣Gather: Passport, Visa, PAN, NRO statement, source-of-funds proof
  • 3️⃣If total FY transfers exceed ₹5 Lakh: obtain Form 15CB from a Chartered Accountant
  • 4️⃣File Form 15CA on incometax.gov.in
  • 5️⃣Submit 15CA acknowledgment + 15CB + proofs to bank
  • 6️⃣Bank debits NRO (principal + TDS + ₹250–1,000 charges + GST)
  • 7️⃣NRE credited within 1–3 working days (same day if intra-bank)
📋
FATCA / CRS — What Agents Must Know
Customers panic here — your job is to reassure
🚨 Most common customer fear"FATCA means I'll be taxed more or reported to the government."
  • 📋FATCA (US law) + CRS (global standard) = identifying customer tax jurisdictions. Reporting only — no new tax
  • ✅NRE interest stays 100% tax-free in India regardless of FATCA
  • 🇬🇧UK TIN = National Insurance Number
  • 🇺🇸US TIN = Social Security Number (SSN) or ITIN
  • 🇦🇪UAE = No TIN required (UAE has no income tax)
  • ⚠️Customers must update FATCA declaration within 30 days of any status change
  • 🚫Agents must NOT determine whether someone is a "US person" or suggest how to answer
📢 "This is a global compliance requirement — every bank in India, the UK, and the US does this. It just means we report to regulators that the account exists. It creates no new tax. Your NRE interest stays completely tax-free."
📊
Tax Rules — Factual Only, No Tax Advice
🚫 Critical ruleAgents must NOT provide tax advice, DTAA guidance, or ITR recommendations. Direct customers to a qualified tax advisor.

⏱ ~15 min
Scripts to memorise
📞
Call Opening Scripts

🧠
The Golden Rule — Acknowledge Before Solving
🚨 Biggest PBM mistakeJumping to the solution before the customer feels heard. If they feel unheard, CSAT suffers even if you solved the problem.
  • 🛑When customer is upset: STOP — acknowledge first, always
  • 💬Say: "You're completely right to feel this way. I'm sorry."
  • 👂Let them finish — never interrupt to give a solution
  • 🪞Mirror: "I understand — 3 weeks is a long time, that's not okay"
  • ✅Only THEN: "Here's what I'm doing right now to fix this..."
Your tone goal"Your dedicated banking contact helping complete an important process" — NOT a sales agent. Sound: calm, confident, consultative, ownership-oriented.
⚡
Objection Handling Scripts
⏰ "Too Busy / No Time"
  • Ask first: "What rate is your bank giving on NRE savings?"
  • Aspora 7% vs HDFC 3% → ₹70,000 MORE per year on ₹20L
  • Ask for 90 seconds — not "2 minutes" (sounds smaller)
  • If still busy → lock exact day + time before hanging up
🏦 "Already Have Another Bank"
  • Never say "close it" — say "add Aspora alongside"
  • HDFC 3% vs Aspora 7% → ₹70,000 more/yr on ₹20L
  • "Most NRIs keep HDFC for bills, Aspora for savings"
  • Complement, don't compete
🔒 "Is It Safe?"
  • RBI regulated (via SSFB) — same as HDFC & ICICI
  • DICGC insured up to ₹5 lakh — government-backed
  • Why 7%: no branches + no ATMs = savings passed to you
  • "IDFC First Bank also offers 7% on the same model"
👨‍👩‍👧 "Need Family Discussion"
  • Send WhatsApp summary immediately — don't wait
  • Ask: "What specific concern will your family have?"
  • Offer a 3-way call with family on the line
  • Lock a specific callback time before hanging up
💳 "What Are the Fees?"
  • Zero fees · Zero minimum balance · Zero annual charges
  • Free international transfers — Year 1
  • HDFC needs ₹10K min → ₹5,000/yr penalty if below
  • 100% free to maintain — no hidden costs
😓 "Process Too Complicated"
  • "You're at step X of 30. I'll guide each remaining step personally."
  • 15–20 minutes total — fully digital, no branch
  • Progress saved automatically — no restart if interrupted
  • Start Step 1 NOW on the call — biggest drop-off reducer

Module 5 — Compliance & Operations

DICGC, beneficiary rules, card management, KYC ops, and the key numbers that come up on calls.

⏱ ~15 min
Know the numbers
🛡
DICGC — Deposit Insurance
Use this to answer "is my money safe?"
  • 🏛DICGC = Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation — statutory body under RBI
  • 🏦All scheduled banks are members — including SSFB (Aspora's banking partner)
  • 💰Covers up to ₹5 lakh per depositor per bank
  • 📊97%+ of individual Indian depositors are fully covered within this limit
  • 💳Banks pay 0.12% of deposits annually as insurance premium
📢 "Same as HDFC or SBI — Government of India backed. DICGC covers your deposits up to ₹5 lakh. It's a statutory requirement for all scheduled banks."
👥
Beneficiary Management Rules
⚠️ New beneficiary cooling periodFirst 24 hours: max ₹50,000/day, maximum 2 transactions only. After 24 hours: full limits apply.
  • ✅New domestic beneficiary: verified via penny drop (5–10 seconds)
  • 🌍International beneficiaries: screened against OFAC/UN sanctions lists
  • 📊Maximum 30 transactions per day across all modes
  • 💸Daily third-party transfer cap: ₹30 lakh
CountryInternational Beneficiary Fields Required

Debit Card Management
  • 📱Virtual card: issued instantly, one per account, RuPay (India domestic only)

  • 🌐International card support: planned Q3 2026 — do not promise to customers
