NRE Account — Deep Dive
This is what Aspora offers
  • 🌍 Holds money earned abroad — converted to INR when deposited
  • 💚 Interest is 100% tax-free in India — zero TDS deducted at source
  • ✅ Fully repatriable — send money back abroad anytime, any amount, no RBI approval
  • 📈 Earns up to 7% savings rate with Aspora (vs 3% at HDFC/ICICI)
  • 🎁 Year 1: free international transfers
  • 💳 Virtual debit card + UPI access via international number
🏠
NRO Account — When a Customer Asks
⚠️ Aspora does not offer NRO accounts — if a customer specifically needs NRO, acknowledge it and focus on who Aspora is best for.
  • 🏠 Used for India-based income — rental income, pension, dividends, interest
  • ⚠️ Interest is taxable in India — TDS deducted
  • 🔒 Repatriation capped at USD 1 million/yr and requires documentation
  • 💬 Many NRIs have both NRE + NRO at different banks — that's fine
🌐
FATCA / CRS — Tax Residency Declaration
Customers panic at this step — you need to calm them
🚨 Common fear: "FATCA means I'll be taxed more or reported to the government."
  • 📋 FATCA/CRS is a global reporting standard — all Indian banks do this, it's not specific to Aspora
  • ✅ Does NOT create any new tax liability — purely a reporting requirement
  • 💚 NRE interest stays completely tax-free in India regardless of FATCA
  • 🇬🇧 UK customers: TIN = National Insurance Number
  • 🇺🇸 US customers: TIN = Social Security Number
  • 🇦🇪 UAE customers: no TIN required — UAE has no income tax
📢 "This is a global compliance requirement — every bank in India, the UK, the US does this. It just means we report to regulators that the account exists. It doesn't change your tax status or create any new tax. Your NRE interest stays tax-free."
💸
How Remittance Works
  • 1️⃣ Customer sends SWIFT/wire from foreign bank → Aspora NRE account
  • 2️⃣ Money converts to INR at mid-market rate
  • 3️⃣ Credited to NRE account — starts earning interest immediately
  • 🎁 Year 1: international transfers are free on Aspora
  • ✅ Repatriation: send back from NRE account — 100% free, unlimited

Module 4 — Call Handling

How to handle objections, empathise with frustrated customers, and close every call with CSAT.

⏱ ~15 minutes
🎯 Objection scripts ready
🧠
The Golden Rule — Acknowledge Before Solving
🚨 Biggest PBM mistake: jumping to the solution before the customer feels heard. If they feel unheard, CSAT suffers even if you solved the problem.
  • 🛑 When customer is upset: STOP — acknowledge first, always
  • 💬 Say: "You're completely right to feel this way. I'm sorry."
  • 👂 Let them finish — never interrupt to give a solution
  • 🪞 Mirror their frustration: "I understand — 3 weeks is a long time, that's not okay"
  • ✅ Only THEN: "Here's what I'm doing right now to fix this..."
⚡
Objection Handling Scripts
⏰ "Too Busy / No Time"
  • 🎯 Ask first: "What rate is your bank giving you on NRE savings?"
  • ✅ Aspora 7% vs HDFC 3% → ₹40,000 extra/yr on ₹10L
  • ⏱ Ask for 90 seconds — not "2 minutes"
  • 📅 If still busy → lock exact day + time before hanging up
🏦 "Already Have Another Bank"
  • ✅ Never say "close it" — say "add Aspora alongside"
  • 💰 ₹20L: Aspora = ₹70,000 MORE per year than HDFC
  • 👥 "Most NRIs keep HDFC for bills, Aspora for savings"
  • 🤝 Complement, don't compete
🔒 "Is It Safe?"
  • 🏛 RBI regulated — same as HDFC & ICICI
  • 🛡 DICGC insured up to ₹5 lakh — govt-backed
  • 💡 Why 7%: no branches + no ATMs = savings passed to you
  • 🏦 "IDFC First Bank also gives 7% — same model"
👨‍👩‍👧 "Need to Discuss with Family"
  • 📲 Send WhatsApp summary immediately — don't wait
  • ❓ "What specific concern will your family have?"
  • 📞 Offer a 3-way call with family on the line
  • 📅 Lock a specific time before hanging up
💳 "What Are the Fees?"
  • ✅ Zero fees · Zero minimum balance · Zero annual charges
  • 🎁 Free international transfers in Year 1
  • ⚠️ HDFC needs ₹10K min → ₹5,000/yr penalty if below
  • 💚 100% free to maintain — no hidden costs
😓 "Process Seems Complicated"
  • 📋 Form → 3 docs → Video KYC → Notary → SIM → Active!
  • ⏱ 15–20 minutes total — fully digital
  • 💬 You guide on WhatsApp through every step
  • 🚀 Start Step 1 NOW on the call — biggest drop-off reducer
📞
When to Call After a Notification (RFI Strategy)
⚠️ Don't call immediately after a notification — the customer hasn't read it yet. You'll sound like spam.
  • ⏱ Wait 1–2 hours after notification before calling
  • 💼 Banking is a long-term relationship — not transactional
  • 📢 Script: "Hi, I saw you received our update — calling to personally help with the next step"
⭐
Closing Every Call — CSAT Script
"Before we close today, I'd like to ask you to complete a quick 1-question satisfaction survey that takes just 10 seconds. It helps us improve the support we give to NRIs like yourself. I'll send it via WhatsApp right now." [Send survey link immediately] "Thank you so much for your time today. Is there anything else I can help you with before we go?"
  • ⭐ Send CSAT on every call — even if the issue wasn't fully resolved
  • 📊 CSAT measures empathy and clarity — not just whether the problem was solved
  • 💚 Your CSAT score = your performance score
  • ⚠️ If customer declines survey: note as declined — it still counts in your pipeline
